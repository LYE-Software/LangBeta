About Train
Train is a scaling, repetition based, memory training mode. It works best when you do not previously know the material being studied.
Train uses three modes to help you ease into your knowledge:
- A learn mode, where you are told both the question and answer
- A multiple choice mode, where you are given multiple options and must choose the correct one
- A write mode, where you must fill in the correct answer.
Train groups questions into smaller groups so that you can learn portions at a time. You can choose the size of these groups.
To ensure you retain the information, Train quizzes you on previous "groups" in later ones.
To keep track of your progress, the bar at the top of the screen will display your progress through the Studysheet as a whole, while the bar at the bottom will show your progress through the current group.
